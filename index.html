Biomedical AI and Digital Health Engineer

I am a Biomedical Engineering postgraduate at the University of Sydney with previous qualifications in Engineering Physics and Electrical Engineering. My experience spans medical image analysis, biomedical signal processing, wearable sensing, deep learning and applied research. I have worked on machine learning systems for chest X-ray analysis, inertial-sensor-based movement assessment, ECG identification and non-invasive physiological sensing. My focus is not only model performance, but also data quality, experimental design, validation, interpretability and practical deployment.
